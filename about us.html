About Us

Established in 2022 by Precious Radebe, Empowering the Nation is dedicated to providing skills training for domestic workers and gardeners in Johannesburg. Our mission is to create opportunities for those who were never given the chance to upskill themselves or follow formal education.

We offer both six-month Learnerships and six-week Short Skills Training Programmes that equip participants with marketable skills, enabling them to command higher wages or start their own small businesses.

Our training is practical, hands-on, and focused on real-world applications. We believe that every person, regardless of background, deserves the chance to grow, contribute, and thrive.

Join us in building a stronger, more empowered community — one skill at a time.
